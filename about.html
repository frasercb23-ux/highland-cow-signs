Our Story

Started in a Shed. Grown with Heart.

Highland Cow Signs Co. started exactly the way you'd hope a Scottish gifts brand would — in a draughty workshop, fuelled by strong tea and a genuine love of those magnificent, fluffy-fringed Highland Cows.

We wanted to make signs and gifts that felt properly Scottish. Not mass-produced trinkets, but pieces with character — made on quality materials, with designs that make people smile and interiors that feel like home.

Every sign we make still gets the same attention it did on day one. We proof every personalised order, hand-check every print, and pack every box ourselves. That won't change.

Frequently Asked

Standard UK delivery is 3–5 working days at £3.99, or free on orders over £35. Express next-day delivery is available at checkout. We despatch Monday to Friday — orders placed before 2pm are usually shipped same day.
All metal signs come pre-drilled with a single hole at the top for easy hanging with a nail or picture hook. Wooden signs come strung with natural jute twine, ready to hang immediately. No tools required.
We offer 30-day returns on all non-personalised items. Simply get in touch and we'll arrange a return. Personalised signs cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or faulty — which is why we send a digital proof before printing.
Use our sign builder to enter your text, choose your style and size, then add to your bag. Once we receive your order we'll email you a digital proof within 24 hours. Once you approve it, we print and despatch within 2–3 working days.
Our standard range is designed for indoor use. The print and finish can be affected by prolonged outdoor exposure and moisture. We do offer a weatherproofed outdoor range — contact us for details.
